Our Values

Respect:

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (DEI) stands at the top of our values. We treat everyone - not limited to our partners (employees/colleagues) and clients - with respect regardless of their age, gender, race, religious beliefs, sexual orientation or disability.

Integrity:

We do what we say we will do and on the rare occasion we cannot, we are transparent. Our clients can depend on, and trust in us.

Fun:

We pride ourselves on having a relaxed and supportive environment. We embrace everyones' talents and encourage each other to excel.

Passion:

We are committed to helping the communities we work within; to contribute and build towards a fun and inclusive society where our partners and clients feel proud to be connected to us.

Excellence:

We are dedicated to producing a top tier service every time. We insist on only the highest standards and everything is done with excellence. This is demonstrated in how we greet our clients, how we speak to each other, and how we clean, the products and equipment that we use.

Our Mission

Transformation:

We are on a mission to redefine and contribute to the shift in the cleaning industry. Cleaning to us is more than wiping your surfaces and emptying your bins, we are committed to Transforming Spaces to Transform Minds.
